About Us

We are a partnership of mechanical engineers and electronic technicians with a passion for producing solar electricity. Our combined technical experience is more than 60 years... and we're still learning!

We install grid-tie solar systems, off-grid solar systems, and grid-tie with battery backup (including the coming new Tesla Power Wall); all total we've installed about 50 different systems from 2kw to 25kw.

We provide many choices for mounting your new solar system. We install roof mounted systems, pole mounted (with or without tracking) and ground mounted systems (with or without tracking).

We have the experience, knowledge and expertise to install the most cost effective and efficient solar system on the market today.

We'd love to hear from you; call us for a free consultation.
